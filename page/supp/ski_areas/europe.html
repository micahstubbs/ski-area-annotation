Ski Areas of the World

Africa Asia Australia / Oceania Europe North America South America
Usage
  • Clicking on one of the links to the right will center the map on that ski area.
  • Annotated ski areas are colored green
  • Unannotated ski areas are colored red
  • The currently selected ski area is colored yellow irregardless of whether it is selected or not
Annotation

If you want to add new ski area names, enter them in the text boxes to the right of the map. To save them, click on 'Export JSON', and store the resulting file (uids-to-names.json) in the appropriate sub-directory:
Africa: /page/jsons/ski-areas/africa/uids-to-names.json
Asia: /page/jsons/ski-areas/asia/uids-to-names.json
Australia / Oceania: /page/jsons/ski-areas/australia-oceania/uids-to-names.json
Europe: /page/jsons/ski-areas/europe/uids-to-names.json
North America: /page/jsons/ski-areas/north-america/uids-to-names.json
South America: /page/jsons/ski-areas/south-america/uids-to-names.json
